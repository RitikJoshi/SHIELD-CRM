The "Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement and Logistics Division" or S.H.I.E.L.D. is a counterterrorism and intelligence agency formerly run by Nick Fury, currently being rebuilt by Phil Coulson. Their famous clients include: Dr.Stephen Strange, Thor Odinson, Peter Parker & several other superheroes.
